Foshan Davis Outdoor Products Co., Ltd.

Founded in 1992, DAVIS Outdoor Products Co., Ltd. is a 33-year manufacturer specializing in the R&D and production of outdoor shading systems. Its product range includes Retractable Awnings, Retractable Roofs, Aluminum Alloy Pergolas, Liftable Screens, Window Awnings, and Roof Roller Blinds.

Operating from a 30,000 sqm factory, the company employs over 450 personnel, including 30 in R&D, 20 in sales, 300 in production, and 100 in installation/maintenance.

DAVIS holds certifications including CE, ISO9001, IONET, SGS, GB-T, UL, and CUL, along with 200 patents. Its products are exported to over 120 countries, primarily in Europe and America, providing OEM and technical services for nearly 300 global brands.
