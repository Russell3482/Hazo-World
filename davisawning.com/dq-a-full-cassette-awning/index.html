Spanish Sauleda Fabric Selection

Sauleda is a globally recognized leader in high-end fabric manufacturing, specializing in outdoor awnings, home decor, marine products, and industrial high-resistance fabrics. With decades of expertise and cutting-edge technology, the group produces top-quality fabrics renowned for their durability, UV resistance, and water pressure performance, all while adhering to strict eco-friendly standards like OEKO-TEX® Standard 100.

Sauleda’s proprietary dyeing technology ensures long-lasting, vibrant colors even under prolonged sun exposure, making its products ideal for demanding environments such as marine applications, where materials must withstand seawater, sunlight, and harsh weather. In outdoor decor, Sauleda’s fabrics combine aesthetic appeal with the ability to endure outdoor conditions, while in the industrial sector, its high-resistance fabrics provide critical protection and safety for equipment.

Trusted by high-end clients across Europe, North America, and Asia, Sauleda Group combines innovation and quality to deliver luxurious, sustainable fabric solutions, setting the benchmark in the industry. Whether for awnings, marine use, outdoor decor, or industrial applications, Sauleda’s products offer reliable performance and exceptional quality, catering to diverse and demanding needs worldwide.

Spanish Sauleda Waterproof Fabric

DUO is a fabric specially designed to withstand adverse weather conditions; it has a natural appearance, an agreeable texture, elegance, design, is lightweight and provides thermal comfort in any type of boat.

DUO is made using raw materials of the highest quality, and a combination of two excellent materials to form a reversible two-sided technical fabric. This innovative process gives it a series of unsurpassable properties and excellent behaviour in marine environments, with outstanding results such as durability, high UV radiation resistance, solid colours that are 100% water-repellent, in addition to the benefit of eliminating condensation and a specific anti-mould treatment for marine environments. It is lightweight and very easy to handle and maintain. Its special design and surface treatment allow the fabric to dry quickly.

5-Year Warranty

The warranty covers abnormal deterioration of the fabric fibre and colour and takes effect from the date of installation, provided there is normal exposure to the sun and atmospheric agents in general. Please consult the warranty terms at www.sauleda.com.

Maintenance

◉Remove dirt by gently brushing or vacuuming the fabric.

◉Occasionally clean the fabric with a solution of water and mild soap (maximum water temperature 30°C).

◉Apply the solution to the fabric and rinse with running water.

◉Allow the fabric to dry completely.

◉Do not use solvents or abrasive detergents.

Barrier Effect

All our acrylic fabrics are treated with SAULEDA FABRIC PROTECTOR. This creates a barrier effect and enhances the water-repellent properties of the fabric.

Additionally, it delays the adherence of dirt to the fabric. It is important to note that factors such as acid rain, bird droppings, or washing the fabric with abrasive products may damage the SAULEDA FABRIC PROTECTOR coating.
