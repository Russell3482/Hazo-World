Modular Building Cabin Space Capsule

Outdoor Space Capsule Lodging is a distinctive and innovative accommodation option that integrates elements of space capsules and outdoor camping, offering travelers an entirely new lodging experience. Equipped with a smart control system, guests can effortlessly manage the temperature, lighting, and sound within the capsule, creating the most comfortable living environment for their stay.

Applicable venue:

◉Mountain ranges, mountain peaks, wilderness, islands, waterside, glaciers, beaches.

◉Forests, valleys, grasslands, parks, plains, and more.

Installation And Transportation

No Restriction On Terrain Ecological Development

No foundation is laid, the peaks, dense forests and tidal flats are covered in all terrains, no plants or trees are destroyed, no construction waste is produced, and it is ecologically friendly.

Modular Building Cabin Quick landing

30 days of production in the workshop and the whole house is ready to use in 2 hours without on-site construction and decoration

Quality Design Attract Attention And Attract Traffic

100% original design, blended into the mountains and wilderness, the product has its own fan traffic

Panoramic View Back To Nature

270° panoramic floor-to-ceiling windows thermal insulation, sleep in the scenery and enjoy pure natural pleasure

Transportation Stage

The standard truck is a 13.5m long semi-trailer with a transport width of 3m and a transport height of no more than 4.2m. It can travel freely on highways across the country.

Hoisting Stage

After the goods arrive at the site, customers need to contact the local crane service themselves. The lifting weight of the product is between 6 and 10 tons. It is recommended to use a crane of no less than 25 tons for loading and unloading.

Installation Phase

Under the guidance of our technicians, we complete the on-site engineering of water supply, electricity supply, sewage pipelines and load-bearing foundations, and accurately place them at the designed installation points. After connecting water and electricity, we complete the water and electricity test and submit them to the customer for acceptance.

MAIN FEATURE

Intelligent Voice System

◉Utilizing an intelligent voice management system, guests can easily control curtains, lighting, climate, air conditioning, and more through voice commands, enhancing the overall stay experience.

All-Terrain Coverage

◉Striving to maintain the original terrain as much as possible, avoiding extensive leveling or altering topography, to preserve the natural landscape of the local surroundings.

Panoramic Views

◉Outdoor space capsule lodging featuring 270° panoramic floor-to-ceiling windows allows guests to enjoy the surrounding beauty in a warm and inviting environment.

Zero Construction Waste

◉Minimizing or recycling construction waste generated during the building process to reduce negative environmental impact.

Ecological Conservation

◉Emphasizing respect and protection for the ecosystem, ensuring that development projects do not cause irreversible harm to local biodiversity and ecological balance.
