Bioclimate Pergola

Aluminum Bioclimate Pergola

A modern all-aluminium pergola system designed for flexible control of sunlight, shade and ventilation. When the louvres are fully closed, they form a sealed roof with concealed drainage, providing reliable rain protection. Built to suit Australian conditions, offering stable performance and long-term durability.

Available in three structural levels:

F168 – Standard residential series
F210 – Increased span and structural strength
F210 PRO – Premium heavy-duty specification for larger or more demanding projects

Retractable Louvered Pergola

A fully aluminium retractable louvre pergola designed for flexible outdoor living. The blades can rotate and retract to one side, allowing you to open up the roof for maximum light and sky exposure, or close it for complete shade and rain protection.

When closed, the system forms a solid roof with integrated drainage to channel water discreetly through the frame. Engineered to withstand Australian weather conditions, it offers long-term durability and reliable structural performance.
