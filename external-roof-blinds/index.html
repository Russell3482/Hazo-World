External Roof Blinds

Fabric Roof Blind

External Roof Blinds is a new type of sun room standard product whose overall structure is different from traditional awnings. The new structure and power system design enable the product to obtain the characteristics of easy installation, good contact effect, low maintenance cost and larger coverage area. It is a subversive sun room shading solution.

Applicable venue:

◉High-end clubs, star hotels, villas and other outdoor places.

◉Terrace, restaurant, swimming pool, garden glass house, private residential area, etc.

Aluminum Alloy Roof Roller Shutters

The aluminum alloy roof roller shutters use aluminum alloy curtains for shading, the system adopts double motor FTS mechanism, the product is more stable, long service life, the heat insulation effect is not only far better than the various indoor sunshade products in the sunroom, it can significantly reduce the indoor temperature.

Applicable venue:

◉High-end clubs, star hotels, villas and other outdoor places.

◉Terrace, restaurant, swimming pool, garden glass house, private residential area, etc.
