Motor Operation Principle

Blade Retraction:

The red motor rotates clockwise, driving the steel belt and blades to rotate, while the blue motor follows.

Blade Extension:

The blue motor rotates clockwise, driving the steel belt and blades to rotate, while the red motor follows.
