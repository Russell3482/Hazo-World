5-Year Warranty

The warranty covers abnormal deterioration of the fabric fibre and colour and takes effect from the date of installation, provided there is normal exposure to the sun and atmospheric agents in general. Please consult the warranty terms at www.sauleda.com.

Maintenance

Remove dirt by gently brushing or vacuuming the fabric.

Occasionally clean the fabric with a solution of water and mild soap (maximum water temperature 30°C).

Apply the solution to the fabric and rinse with running water.

Allow the fabric to dry completely.

Do not use solvents or abrasive detergents.

Barrier Effect

All our acrylic fabrics are treated with SAULEDA FABRIC PROTECTOR. This creates a barrier effect and enhances the water-repellent properties of the fabric.

Additionally, it delays the adherence of dirt to the fabric. It is important to note that factors such as acid rain, bird droppings, or washing the fabric with abrasive products may damage the SAULEDA FABRIC PROTECTOR coating.
