HAZO WOLRD PTY LTD.

Established in Australia, HAZO WORLD is a trade-only supplier of architectural outdoor systems, specialising in fabric shading systems, bioclimatic pergolas and retractable roof structures. We work exclusively with wholesalers, fabrication partners and distribution networks, delivering system-based solutions rather than single products.

Through long-term equity participation and operational involvement, we are closely aligned with core manufacturing facilities, ensuring consistency in product standards, quality control and scalable production capacity. Our integrated supply model supports both complete systems and component-level supply.
