Advantage

Remote-controlled retractable system, supports synchronous operation of multiple devices.

T6063-T5 aluminum alloy offers excellent oxidation and corrosion resistance.

The fabric has excellent UV resistance, is not prone to fading, and is resistant to staining.

Designed with a high-density microporous structure and precision weaving, it offers a unique one-way vision effect. From the outside, it provides privacy with a concealed view, while from the inside, it ensures clarity and high light transmission.

This fabric effectively blocks sun and rain, with its shading and waterproof performance determined by the pore size.The SL00 Full Blackout Series achieves 100% rainproof and sunproof protection.

The fabric surface can clearly display projected images, making it suitable for meetings, presentations, or home use. It combines shading with multifunctional display capabilities, enhancing both space efficiency and practicality.

Dooya Motor Introduction

Leading Smart Home and Motor Solutions Provider - Dooya was established in the early 2000s, headquartered in Ningbo, China, with a presence in over 60 countries and regions worldwide. Its products are widely used in more than 100 countries and regions. Dooya has established R&D centers in China, Europe, and the United States, and holds over 130 patents to date.

For over two decades, Dooya has been dedicated to collaborating with global partners, including smart home integrators, architects, and designers. Through innovative products such as smart motors, automated window systems, and intelligent shading solutions, Dooya strives to enhance energy efficiency, improve living comfort, and create smarter, more sustainable living spaces for users worldwide.

A-OK Motor Introduction

Established in 1998, A-OK specializes in designing and manufacturing automation and motorization systems for shading, windows, and doors. Over the years, the company has expanded to include divisions such as Precision Rotor & Stator, Precision Metal Stamping, Precision Plastic Tooling and Injection, Precision Gear Transmission, and Mechanical & Electronic Control, ensuring sustainable development and innovation in its products and services.

A-OK offers a comprehensive range of products, including AC and DC tubular motors, curtain motors, Venetian blinds motors, window openers, and various smart scene control systems. The company adheres to the management concept of "high standard, refinement, and zero defect," and its products have earned certifications such as CE, TUV, UL, cTUVus, FCC, NF, CCC, and RoHS, demonstrating their commitment to quality and international standards.
