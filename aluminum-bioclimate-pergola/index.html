Aluminum Bioclimate Pergola

A modern all-aluminium pergola system designed for flexible control of sunlight, shade and ventilation. When the louvres are fully closed, they form a sealed roof with concealed drainage, providing reliable rain protection. Built to suit Australian conditions, offering stable performance and long-term durability.

Available in three structural levels:

F168 – Standard residential series
F210 – Increased span and structural strength
F210 PRO – Premium heavy-duty specification for larger or more demanding projects
