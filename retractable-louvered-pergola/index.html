Retractable Louvered Pergola

The retractable louvered pergola is an architectural masterpiece made of full aluminum, capable of various form transformations. The blades can be fully folded and retracted to one end. When the blades are completely retracted, the translucent space can reach 75%, allowing you to enjoy the panoramic sky. When closed, it provides shade from direct sunlight and blocks rainwater. The material is resistant to harsh weather and environments, making it sturdy and durable, with a lifespan of up to 20 years.

Applicable venue:

◉High-end clubs, star hotels, villas, squares and other outdoor places.

◉Terraces, restaurants, swimming pools, gardens, private residential areas, etc.

A-OK Motor Introduction

Established in 1998, A-OK specializes in designing and manufacturing automation and motorization systems for shading, windows, and doors. Over the years, the company has expanded to include divisions such as Precision Rotor & Stator, Precision Metal Stamping, Precision Plastic Tooling and Injection, Precision Gear Transmission, and Mechanical & Electronic Control, ensuring sustainable development and innovation in its products and services.

A-OK offers a comprehensive range of products, including AC and DC tubular motors, curtain motors, Venetian blinds motors, window openers, and various smart scene control systems. The company adheres to the management concept of "high standard, refinement, and zero defect," and its products have earned certifications such as CE, TUV, UL, cTUVus, FCC, NF, CCC, and RoHS, demonstrating their commitment to quality and international standards.
