Retractable Roof

It is one of the most popular outdoor space expansion solutions. It is suitable for large outdoor spaces. The fabric stretches and moves with the aluminum track through a special motor. When it is opened, customers can feel the sunshine and air of nature. After closing, it can be 100% waterproof and effectively block the sun. It is very good in terms of heat insulation effect and aesthetics.

Applicable venue:

◉High-end clubs, star hotels, villas, squares and other outdoor places.

◉Terraces, restaurants, swimming pools, gardens, private residential areas, etc.

Advantage

PVC fabric is completely waterproof and corrosion-resistant

Drainage outlets at the base of hollow posts

Integrated LED ambient lighting strips

The water guard panel effectively blocks leaves and dust while preventing rainwater damage to the motor and shade fabric, providing reliable protection and maintaining a clean, aesthetically pleasing appearance.

The guide rails feature polyurethane automotive-grade rubber rain seals that are seamlessly integrated with the frame, directing rainwater into the gutter system.

The corrosion-resistant 6063-T5 aluminum alloy guide rails channel water into the gutters, which then route the rainwater through hollow posts for effective perimeter drainage.
